Who Is This For?

What's inside?

7 Practical, Easy-to-Follow Modules:

  1. Attaching to Block Walls - Secure pictures, shelves, and even heavy items with confidence.
  2. Attaching to Stud Walls - Learn what to watch out for and how these differ from block walls.
  3. Plasterboard Fixings - No stud in the right place? No problem! Master plasterboard anchors for strong, reliable wall fixings.
  4. Fixing Damaged Areas - Mistakes happen! Learn to repair and restore so you never have to fear DIY projects again.
  5. Introduction to Kitchens - Adjust cabinet doors like a pro for a sleek, polished look.
  6. Internal Doors - Replace damaged locks and adjust doors for smooth opening and closing.
  7. Upcycling Kitchens - Transform your kitchen using hand tools and creativity!
