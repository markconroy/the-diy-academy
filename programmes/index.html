About the one-day live Workshops

Join the DIY Academy for a fun-filled day of learning and hands-on DIY projects tailored specifically for women.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, this workshop is the perfect place to start.

Here you will get hands on experience with tools and products, get familiar with essential home DIY skills ,enjoy a delicious lunch and meet some other DIY enthusiasts and of course a goodie bag to take home!

With both Beginner and Advanced workshops available it's a fully immersive experience!

Absolutely no experience necessary with all materials provided!

Beginners workshop schedule

  • Drills 101
  • Block walls, fixings and bits
  • Stud walls, plasterboard and filler
  • Lunch
  • Fixing damaged areas
  • Hanging pictures
  • Painting

Intermediate workshop schedule

  • Jigsaw 101
  • Shelving
  • Plumbing basics
  • Doors locks and hinges
  • Kitchens 101

Online Beginners Course:

Can't make it to our live sessions? No problem! Our comprehensive online course brings The DIY Academy to your fingertips.

Join the next round of our beginners DIY online course on 16th November 2024 aimed to empower you with valuable skills and knowledge of DIY home improvements that you can apply from the comfort of your home.

WHAT'S INCLUDED:

ONLINE VIDEO SERIES:

Receive practical and informative content that covers the core concepts and practical applications of basic home improvements through easy to digest videos.

Covering six modules this is a complete online learning experience allowing you to pace your learning according to your schedule.

ACCESS TO AN EXCLUSIVE ONLINE COMMUNITY:

Join our private facebook community with other DIY enthusiasts. This is a safe space to share your DIY projects you have been working on, any questions you have that you want answered and regular live Q&A sessions for further support to enhance your online DIY journey.

THIS IS FOR YOU IF

  • You are sick of waiting for jobs to get done around your home
  • You want to save money by learning to do the smaller jobs yourself
  • You want to transform your living space
  • You are looking for new outlets to express your creativity
  • You are ready to discover new passions and grow your capabilities

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

  • Discover the best techniques for hanging pictures on your walls.
  • Master the Drill: Learn to use a drill correctly and safely.
  • Become familiar and learn the different products and tools you can put to use in your home
  • Lock & Hinge Repairs: Gain the skills to fix locks and hinges on doors and cabinets efficiently.
  • Shelving Installation: Learn to install shelves securely and stylishly
  • How to identify and fix common DIY errors
