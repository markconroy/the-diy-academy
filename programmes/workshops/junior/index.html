Junior DIY Workshops

Building Confidence, Creativity & Practical Skills

Our Junior DIY Workshops are designed to introduce children to the world of DIY in a safe, hands-on and confidence-building way. These workshops give young people aged between 8 and 14 the opportunity to learn practical skills, develop problem-solving abilities and experience the satisfaction of creating something with their own hands.

Perfect for curious minds who love to build, make and learn by doing.

Upcoming Junior Workshops

What Children Will Learn

During our Junior DIY Workshops, participants will:

  • Learn the safe use of basic hand tools
  • Practice measuring, marking, cutting, sanding and assembling
  • Develop coordination, focus and problem-solving skills
  • Build and personalise their own DIY project to take home
  • Gain confidence through hands-on learning in a supportive environment

No prior experience is needed - everything is taught step by step.

Find a Workshop

Who These Workshops Are For

  • Children aged 8–14
  • Kids who enjoy hands-on, practical activities
  • Young people who learn best by doing
  • Parents looking for a screen-free, skill-based experience

We do encourage parents or guardians to stay and share the experience with their child.

A Safe & Supportive Environment

Safety is a top priority at all Junior DIY Workshops.

  • All tools and materials are provided
  • Clear safety guidance is given throughout
  • Workshops are led by experienced instructors
  • Group sizes are kept small to ensure individual attention

Children are encouraged to ask questions, try new skills and learn at their own pace.

What’s Included

  • Guided DIY workshop
  • All tools and materials
  • A completed project to bring home
  • A confidence-boosting learning experience

(Workshops also include lunch - individual details provided per event.)
