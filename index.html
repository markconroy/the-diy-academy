Meet Your Instructor

Hi, I'm Martin Glynn

At The DIY Academy, my philosophy is that everyone should have the confidence and know-how to tackle common household projects.

My mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to create, repair, and enhance their living spaces – without needing to wait for professionals. With over 20 years of experience and a commitment to hands-on learning, I am here to guide you every step of the way.
